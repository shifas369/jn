Experiencing Kerala

Athirappilly Falls, is situated in Athirappilly Panchayat in Chalakudy Taluk of Thrissur District in Kerala, India on the Chalakudy

Ponmudi is also famous as an adventure site,The best time to visit Ponmudi is from October to March.

Kalaripayattu is a martial art designed for the ancient battlefield (the word "Kalari" meaning "battlefield")

Kerala cuisine offers a multitude of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes prepared using fish, poultry and red meat with rice as a typical accompaniment

Nestled in the elaborate forests of the majestic Western Ghats or Sahyadri Ranges in Kerala are fourteen wildlife sanctuaries and two tiger reserves.
